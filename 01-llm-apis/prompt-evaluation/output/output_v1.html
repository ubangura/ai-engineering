# Nutrition Plan for Marathon Training (Vegan & Gluten-Free)

## Daily Calorie & Macro Targets
- **Calories**: ~2,800-3,200/day (adjust based on training volume)
- **Protein**: 110-135g/day (1.6-2g per kg body weight)
- **Carbs**: 420-500g/day (6-7.5g per kg for endurance training)
- **Fat**: 78-100g/day (25-30% of calories)

## Key Foods by Category

### Protein Sources (aim for 25-30g per meal)
- Lentils, chickpeas, black beans
- Tofu, tempeh, edamame
- Pea protein powder
- Hemp seeds, pumpkin seeds
- Nutritional yeast
- Vegan protein pasta

### Carbohydrates (fuel for endurance)
- **Gluten-free grains**: Rice, quinoa, corn, millet, oats
- **Starches**: Sweet potatoes, regular potatoes, tapioca
- **Fruits**: Bananas, dates, berries
- **Rice cakes, gluten-free bread**

### Healthy Fats
- Nuts: almonds, walnuts, cashews
- Seeds: chia, flax, sunflower
- Avocado, olive oil, coconut oil
- Tahini

### Micronutrients (vegan athletes need monitoring)
- **B12**: Fortified plant milks, supplements
- **Iron**: Lentils, spinach, quinoa (with vitamin C for absorption)
- **Calcium**: Fortified plant milk, leafy greens
- **Omega-3s**: Flax/chia seeds, algae supplements

## Sample Day Structure

| Meal | Example |
|------|---------|
| **Breakfast** | Gluten-free oats + banana + almond butter + hemp seeds |
| **Snack** | Dates + almonds |
| **Lunch** | Quinoa + roasted chickpeas + sweet potato + olive oil |
| **Pre-workout** | Rice cakes + peanut butter |
| **Post-workout** | Smoothie: plant milk + pea protein + fruit |
| **Dinner** | Tofu stir-fry + rice + vegetables |

## Marathon-Specific Timing
- **3-4 hours pre-run**: Carbs + protein + minimal fat
- **During long runs (90+ min)**: 30-60g carbs/hour (energy gels, dates, sports drink)
- **Within 30 min post-run**: 1:3-1:4 protein:carb ratio for recovery

**Recommendation**: Consult a sports dietitian familiar with vegan nutrition to optimize performance and prevent deficiencies.
